Welcome to Multi-Player

This page is a video experiment showing the same video multiple times with a little delay between each. A few things to note before you get started:

  • Flashing Lights Warning: The nature of the delays between videos causes a flashing lights effect.
  • The videos loads fastest in Firefox.
  • Google Chrome can take several seconds depending on the size of your screen.
  • Synchronization in the Safari browser often gets out of whack.
  • The videos come from YouTube. A link to each one is provided if you want to bookmark them there.
