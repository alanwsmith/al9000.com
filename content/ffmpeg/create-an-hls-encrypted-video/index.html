I'm looking into encrypting the videos to try to keep them from being slurped up by bots. One thing I looked at is using HLS videos with encryption. I don't think it's going to do what I want. The process involves setting up a playlist that's encrypted and then pointing to a key to decrypt them. The problem is that path to the key is in the playlist. I expect bots will just pick it right up. Regardless, I do like the HLS approach (minus the encryption). It lets you split a video into multiple files and then the browser stitches them together. That provides a better experience when skipping around a video because later chunks can be loaded if they get scrubbed to prior to the full video being downloaded.
