Reminders

  • You can combine lower power books/tools into higher level ones on an anvil
  • If you break a bookshelf without silk touch you have to rebuild it
  • Mine obsidian to get the recipe for the enchanting table
  • Enchanting tables need 15 bookshelves for max effect
  • Open your inventory to keep the game from pausing when you tab out while in single player mode.
  • Plant birch trees with at least two blocks between them. They'll grow if there's only one, but they won't drop enough saplings to sustain.
