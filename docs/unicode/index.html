Unicode Characters

Warning

  • This page generates over 120K unicode character glyphs.
  • The button below starts the process. It can take a few minutes depending on your device.
  • While the output is done in chunks, it can still bog down a browser tab. It may become unresponsive.
  • Clicking on a character copies it.
  • More Details
    • Different fonts include different sets of character glyphs. The page attempts to remove those that are unavailable on your device. This currently works best in Google Chrome, okay in Safari, and not at all in Firefox.
    • Missing character glyphs show up as box with four letters/numbers split into two rows on Firefox.
